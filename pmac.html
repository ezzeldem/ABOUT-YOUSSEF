Print Media Academy

Print Media Academy Cairo was established in 1999, as the Middle East branch of Print Media Academy, Heidelberg Germany. Serving Egypt, the Middle East, and North Africa .

PMAC is part of Youssef Allam Group - a reputable leader in the fields of Printing, Paper and Healthcare .

PMAC is the training and consultancy arm of the group, capable of delivering both practical and theoretical courses with the highest quality and efficient courses .

PMAC in Cairo is one of the largest branches of Heidelberg's Training Academies around the world, which was established on 3000 square meter in Nasr City Free Zone in Cairo.
